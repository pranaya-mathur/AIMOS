# SaaS & Tech Vertical Specialization
## Industry Nuance
- **Problem-Agitation-Solution**: Direct fit with the software's unique utility or integration ecosystem.
- **Trust Badges**: Integration icons (Slack, Zapier, etc.) and customer logos are essential.
- **Low Friction**: "No Credit Card Required" or "Get Started in 60 Seconds" are high-value hooks.

## Industry Benchmarks (Predictive)
- **Avg CTR**: 1.8% - 2.5%
- **Avg CPL**: $40 - $120 (depending on ACV)
- **Top Visuals**: Dashboard screenshots, 3D abstractions, founder-led video.
- **Top Hooks**: "Stop wasting time on X", "Scale your Y without Z".
- **Safety**: High trademark sensitivity.

## Conversion-Ready Tactics
- **CTA**: Use "Get Your Free Demo", "Start Free Trial", or "See It in Action".
- **Hook**: "Stop [Problem] Forever" or "Scale Your [Function] with [Product Name]".
- **Lead Capture**: Corporate Email address and Company Size.
