# Real Estate Vertical Specialization
## Industry Nuance
- **Urgency**: Market conditions (rates, inventory) drive action.
- **Trust**: Neighborhood expertise is the primary differentiator.
- **Visuals**: Professional photography and "Virtual Tours" are high-value lead magnets.

## Conversion-Ready Tactics
- **CTA**: Use "Schedule a Private Showing", "Get the Neighborhood Report", or "View Floor Plans".
- **Hook**: "Just Listed in [Neighborhood]" or "Don't Miss This [Street Name] Gem".
- **Lead Capture**: Priority is Phone Number + Immediate Follow-up expectations.

## Industry Benchmarks (Predictive)
- **Avg CTR**: 2.0% - 3.5%
- **Avg CPL**: $15 - $45 (Buyer leads)
- **Top Visuals**: High-end interior wide angles, pool/drone shots, lifestyle location shots.
- **Top Hooks**: "Wait until you see the kitchen", "Off-market gems in [City]".
- **Safety**: Fair Housing Act compliance (no race/age targeting in copy).
